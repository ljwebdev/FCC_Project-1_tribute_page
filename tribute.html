John Conway

Mathemetician and inventer of the Game of Life

John Conway was a mathemetician and inventor of the Game of Life. The Game of Life is a simple computer program that simulates the life cycle of single cell organisms based on four simple rules:

  1. A live cell with with fewer than three neighbors becomes a dead cell
  2. A live cell with two or three neighbors lives on to the next cycle
  3. A live cell with four neighbors will become a dead cell, due to overcrowding
  4. A dead cell with exactly three neighbors will become a live cell
